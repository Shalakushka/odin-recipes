Sushi Recipe

Description

The word sushi 寿司 refers to a Japanese dish consisting of vinegared rice called shari assembled with other ingredients called neta which are usually raw fish and seafood . It can also be vegetables and mushrooms.

Ingredients

  • Sushi Rice
  • Fresh Fish
  • Seaweed Sheets (Nori)
  • Rice vinegar

Steps

  1. Cook sushi rice.
  2. Season the sushi rice with rice vinegar.
  3. Place a sheet of seaweed on a clean fat surface.
  4. Spread the rice on the sheet and then slices of fish.
  5. Roll the sushi tightly.
  6. Cut the sushi roll.
  7. Enjoy !
